Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how Clarity handles your information. As this extension does not collect any personal data from users, there is no information to be stored or shared with third parties; therefore, your privacy is fully protected. Clarity does not collect any personally identifiable information. The API key and the “language” are all stored locally and are in no way shared with Clarity. By using Clarity, you agree to this policy.

About Us

This team in the Congressional App Challenge is based out of Portsmouth, New Hampshire. They attend Portsmouth High School and all value education and its importance. This app was developed as a tool to allow learners of varying comprehension levels to find any online text accessible and enjoyable.
